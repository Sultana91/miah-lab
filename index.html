We study...

In the Koss lab, the primary interest is understanding how the immune system controls cancer progression. Active areas of investigation include:

  • Proteome turnover dynamics
  • CAR-T cell therapies
  • Metabolic stress in solid tumors
  • Patient and population immune monitoring

We are interested in...

Discovering new ways to harness the immune system for the treatment of cancer with a primary focus on solid tumors.

We are multi-disciplinary...

Our expertise includes cancer biology, adaptive immunity, mouse models of cancer, proteomics, and bioinformatics. We employ a wide range of techniques to rigorously challenge our ideas.

Welcome to the Miah lab

Mentoring and training future generations of cancer researchers is a top priority of the Koss Lab. Our goal is to help each and every lab member enjoy daily life in the lab as well as reach their maximum potential. We seek to maintain a wide range of expertise in the lab and are interested in talking with anyone interested in the following areas of research:

  • Cancer biology
  • Immunology
  • Bioinformatics
  • Population Sciences
  • Proteomics
  • Cell engineering
  • Metabolism
